# Bobby Brock Insurance - Site Structure

## Directory Structure

### Core Pages
- `/about` - Company information and leadership
- `/team` - Team member profiles
- `/contact` - Contact information and forms
- `/privacy-policy` - Legal documentation

### Medicare Education
- `/medicare`
  - `/basics` - Foundational Medicare knowledge
  - `/eligibility` - Qualification requirements
  - `/parts` - Medicare components
    - `/part-a` - Hospital coverage
    - `/part-b` - Medical coverage
    - `/part-c` - Medicare Advantage
    - `/part-d` - Prescription coverage
  - `/supplement` - Medigap plans
  - `/compare` - Plan comparison tools
  - `/faqs` - Frequently asked questions

### Insurance Plans
- `/plans`
  - `/advantage` - Medicare Advantage plans
    - `/hmo` - HMO plan details
    - `/ppo` - PPO plan details
  - `/supplement` - Medigap coverage options
  - `/prescription` - Part D plans
  - `/dental` - Dental coverage
  - `/vision` - Vision coverage

### Resources
- `/resources`
  - `/guides` - Educational materials
  - `/calculators` - Cost estimation tools
  - `/glossary` - Medicare terminology
  - `/state-specific` - State-based information

### Dynamic Content
- `/blog/[slug]` - Blog articles
- `/videos/watch/[slug]` - Video content
